HOW TO PLAY

Compete in a series of athletic events against your friends!

GENERAL CONTROLS

LEFT / RIGHT Alternate to perform actions
SPACE Special action (event-specific)
ENTER Confirm

LADDER CLIMBING

LEFT / RIGHT Alternate to climb rungs
TIP Keep alternating evenly to stay balanced!

BOOT THROWING

SPACE (tap) Stop the needle to set spin
SPACE (hold) Charge power, release to throw
TIP More spin = higher multiplier but harder to land!

ROCK SKIPPING

SPACE (tap) Set throw angle, then tap to skip on each bounce
SPACE (hold) Charge power, release to throw
TIP Low angles skip best! Timing gets tighter each bounce!

SOCCER OVER HOUSE

SPACE Set kick angle and charge power
LEFT / RIGHT Sprint around the house, then position for the catch
TIP Clear the roof closely for bonus points! Arrive early!

Each event has unique controls shown before the event starts.
